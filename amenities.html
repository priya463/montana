    • Amenities

    • Custom designed rooms

      Every single one of our rooms is special so that all of our guests have a boutique hotel experience. All rooms boast luxurious bathrooms, hand picked vintage furniture bought in Amien, and handmade beds for unbelievable comfort.

    • Room service

      Whether you want breakfast in bed, or crave a nightcap–we've got you covered.

    • Connecting rooms

      Our rooms are highly adaptable. We love making space for guests. The more the merrier. Ask us about our combined room possibilities.

    • Handmade dreams

      Nothing is more relaxing than a great night's sleep. We offer each of our guests a custom, handmade bed created exclusively for Vesper Hotel by Dutch furnituremakers Softline.

    • High speed internet

      Who likes slow wi-fi? We've made sure all our rooms come with a fast connection, so you can slow down.

    • Car parking

      Parking is available at the boulevard in front of the hotel. You can obtain a parking permit at the reception for € 7,50 per car per night.

    • Complementary breakfast

      Vesper Hotel offers all our guests a full breakfast every morning in our beautiful lobby. Our chef is always available to cater to specific requests.

    • Incredible views

      Nearly all our rooms have a seaside view, and the ones that don't aren't that bad either.

    • Laundry & dry cleaning

      We offer 24-hour laundry & dry cleaning services.

    • Our lobby

      We consider our lobby the heart of our hotel. It's where we celebrate a day well spent and get ready for the next one.

      We serve exquisite cocktails and fine finger foods there, as well as beautiful wines right from our wine cellar. The lobby is the perfect place to experience Noordwijk's famous sunset with a drink, or enjoy the sunrise with a hearty breakfast. Join us.

    • Beauty Treatments

      Get a luxury treatment by visiting the nearby Azzurro Beauty Centre

    • Byredo

      All our rooms are stocked with Byredo cosmetics.

    • Beach venues

      Vesper Hotel has two locations on the beach to enjoy: beach club Bries, where we serve food and entertainment, and Zuiderbad, our family friendly seaside restaurant.
